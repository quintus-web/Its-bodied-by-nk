Our Story

Born from Passion,

"Every woman deserves to feel like the most beautiful version of herself — and I make that happen."

NK Beauty — formerly Itsbodied by NK — is Nairobi's premier luxury beauty destination. Founded by Cynthia Nduta, a passionate beauty expert and entrepreneur, the brand has grown into one of Kenya's most celebrated beauty houses. From precision wig installations to breathtaking bridal transformations, every service is delivered with surgical artistry and an unwavering eye for perfection.

Based in Nairobi, NK Beauty serves clients across Kenya who want more than a beauty appointment — they want an experience.
